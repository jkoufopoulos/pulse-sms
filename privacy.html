Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 20, 2026

Pulse SMS ("Pulse," "we," "us") is an SMS-based service that recommends New York City events and nightlife. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

When you text Pulse, we receive:

  • Your phone number — provided by your carrier via Twilio, our SMS delivery provider.
  • Your message content — the text you send us (e.g., a neighborhood name, "more," or a filter like "comedy").

We do not collect your name, email, location data, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your requests. Your phone number identifies your session so we can send back relevant event recommendations and maintain conversation context (e.g., remembering your neighborhood across follow-up messages).
  • To process your messages. Message content is sent to AI services (Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini) to understand your request and generate responses. These services process your message text only — your phone number is not sent to AI providers.

Data Retention

  • Session data (your neighborhood, recent picks, and conversation context) is stored in temporary server memory and automatically expires after 2 hours of inactivity.
  • We do not maintain a database of phone numbers. Once your session expires, we have no record of your phone number.
  • Message logs may be retained in server logs for up to 7 days for debugging purposes, after which they are automatically deleted.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or message content with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not track your location. Neighborhood information comes only from what you tell us.
  • We do not send unsolicited messages. You will only receive texts in direct response to messages you send us.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Pulse:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery. Twilio processes your phone number and message content to deliver texts. Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI processing for understanding messages and composing responses. Receives message text only. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini) — AI processing for message routing. Receives message text only. Google Privacy Policy
  • Railway — Cloud hosting. Railway Privacy Policy

Opting Out

You can stop receiving messages from Pulse at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Reply HELP for support information.

Children's Privacy

Pulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
