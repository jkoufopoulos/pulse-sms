FAQ

The stuff you're wondering

Is this free?

Yes. Pulse is free to use. Standard text message rates from your carrier apply, but we don't charge anything.

Will you spam me?

No. You only get texts in direct response to messages you send. We never text first. Reply STOP anytime to opt out.

What do you do with my number?

We use it to maintain your session for 2 hours. After that, it's gone. We don't store numbers, sell data, or send marketing.
